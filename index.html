Research

My research focuses on computer vision, particularly 3D computer vision, 3D reconstruction, monocular depth estima- tion, and multi-task learning. Additionally, I delve into domain adaptation techniques to bridge the gap across diverse visual domains. I am also fascinated by generative models and their applications in visual content synthesis.
