The-Hiep Nguyen

I am a Research Member at AITechLab, advised by Dr. Duc Dung Nguyen , working on 3D Computer Vision and Generative Models. I received my B.E. in Computer Science from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), Vietnam.

Email  /  Scholar  /  Github

Research

My research focuses on computer vision, particularly 3D computer vision, 3D reconstruction, monocular depth estima- tion, and multi-task learning. Additionally, I delve into domain adaptation techniques to bridge the gap across diverse visual domains. I am also fascinated by generative models and their applications in visual content synthesis.

ULDepth: Transform Self-supervised Depth Estimation to Unpaired Multi-domain Learning
Phan Thi Huyen Thanh*, Trung Thai Tran*, The Hiep Nguyen, Minh Huy Vu Nguyen, Tran Vu Pham, Truong Vinh Truong Duy, Duc Dung Nguyen
IEEE Open Journal of Signal Processing, 2025

Propose a plug-in framework for unpaired multi-domain learning, enhancing self-supervised depth estimation with adversarial domain situations and dynamic normalization across diverse weather and lighting conditions.

DepthSegNet24: A Label-Free Model for Robust Day-Night Depth and Semantics
Phan Thi Huyen Thanh*, The Hiep Nguyen*, Minh Huy Vu Nguyen, Trung Thai Tran, Tran Vu Pham, Duc Dung Nguyen, Truong Vinh Truong Duy, Natori Naotake
ACCV, 2024

Introduce a label-free day–night model that unifies self-supervised depth estimation and knowledge distillation of semantic segmentation to achieve consistent all-day perception.

MLDSE: Multi-task Learning for Depth and Segmentation Estimation
Nhat Huy Tran Hoang, The Hiep Nguyen, Minh Huy Vu Nguyen, Trung Thai Tran, Xuan Huy Le, Duc Dung Nguyen
RIVF, 2023

Utilize cross-task feature propagation and multi-scale supervision through a single encoder–decoder framework to optimize depth and segmentation tasks.

MonoVINI: Seeing in the Dark with Virtual-World Supervision
Minh Huy Vu Nguyen, Trung Thai Tran, The Hiep Nguyen, Duc Dung Nguyen
RIVF, 2023

Introduce virtual-world supervision, combining realistic simulation data with adversarial domain adaptation to tackle the challenges of nighttime condition.

DarkMDE: Excavating Synthetic Images for Nighttime Depth Estimation Using Cross-Domain Supervision
Trung Thai Tran, Xuan Huy Le, Minh Huy Vu Nguyen, The Hiep Nguyen, Nhat Huy Tran Hoang, Duc Dung Nguyen
ICIT, 2023

Popose a GAN-based framework that uses paired synthetic night and real day images to enforce depth consistency and improve robustness in nighttime scenes.
